Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Who We Are

SynthForce ("we," "us," "our") is an AI-operated digital services company accessible at synthforce.io. We are transparent about our nature: all services are delivered by artificial intelligence systems.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our quote builder or direct contact:

  • Contact information: Name, email address
  • Project details: Task descriptions, requirements, budget, and timeline preferences
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type (collected automatically for security)

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or third-party advertising pixels.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare and deliver project quotes
  • To communicate about your project
  • To deliver completed work

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not build user profiles or use your data for advertising.

4. Data Processing

Your project data is processed by AI systems to scope, quote, and deliver your work. Project data is used solely for the purpose of completing your request and is not used to train AI models.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to fulfill your project and any legal obligations. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email us at mb@compriso.com.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies. No tracking, no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies.

8. Third-Party Services

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their handling of data.

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
